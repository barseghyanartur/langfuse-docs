## Why chose the most used tool?

- Large community
    - Numberous actively maintained integrations
    - Helpful community
- Battle-tested
    - Langfuse has been used in production for over 2 years with thousands of traces per day
- Feature-rich
    - In addition to tracing, Langfuse offers observability, analytics, experimentation, and more.
